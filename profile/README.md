# Touch Soccer Multiverse
TSM Is an **Touch Football League**, one of the top 10 leagues of **Touch Football**, this orgnization is for the **New TSM Website Project** Made by [Rawan](https://github.com/PoopsockPy)

## What Does TSM Website Offers
- **Teams Ranking:** Teams Ranking Are Used For World Cup and many other, also higher team rank means the team is stronger
- **Players Database:** TSM Has Players Database, Which Are: Nationaly, Current Team, Position, Stats, Awards, Contract History and more...
- **Teams:** TSM Has 36 Teams From Divsion 1, 2, 3, And Every Team has it own players names and manager
- **Matches:** TSM Website shows you all season 6 matches: `Note: The match dates might be wrong sooo yeah`
- **Sanctions Database:** TSM Has Sanctions Database with 3 levels of sanction are:
  - **Black Sanction:** Black Sanction which are something very dangerous that the user did, like: death threats, doxxing and more...
  - **Red Sanction:** Red Sanction which are something dangerous and violates most of leagues rules like: cheating, illegal fastflags
  - **Yellow Sanction:** Yellow Sanction which are something normal like: sharing accounts, n word with hard r
Also **Sanctions Database** helps other leagues to ban this kind of people
